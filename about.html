Sai Akash Gopaluni

Born in Kurnool, India and now based in Boston, USA, ‘Kranti’ Gopaluni works as a scientist in the biotech industry. His professional life is rooted in data, experiments, and detail, but his imagination has long been shaped by the stories and figures of Indian Itihasa.

Dashanan: Ādi is his first novel, conceived as a tribute to Dashanan and to the fierce devotion of the Shiva Tandava Stotram. In it, he weaves together a modern scientific lens with ancient epic tradition. Outside of work and writing, Kranti enjoys hiking and camping, drawing inspiration from time spent in nature.
